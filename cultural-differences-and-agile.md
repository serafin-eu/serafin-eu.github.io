As we are all aware, Erin Meyer's *The Culture Map* is a LinkedIn favorite regarding cultural differences.

Below a simple table mapping specific Agile principles against Meyer's eight scales.

| Agile Principle | Cultural Dimension | The Conflict |
|---|---|---|
| Self-Organizing Teams | Egalitarian vs. Hierarchical | Teams waiting for ""The Boss"" to give permission instead of deciding. |
| Pivoting / Flexibility | Linear-time vs. Flexible-time | Difficulty managing Sprints when deadlines are viewed as "suggestions." |
| Transparency | Direct vs. Indirect Negative Feedback | "The Sprint is on track" (while the code is actually on fire) to avoid shame. |
